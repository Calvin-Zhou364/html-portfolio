The Best Movies According to Calvin

My top 3 movies

Kung Fu Panda 2

The series is about a panda and his journey of self discovery and becoming the dragon warrior. I loved the story and how goofy it was. The second one is my favorite because of how deep and dark the story got. We got to learn more about the main character's backstory and it is sad.

Captain America: Civil War

The movie is about the marvel superheroes and two teams fighting due disagreements with their ideals. I have always been a fan of superhero movies, and this is one of my favorites. This was also the first movie I watched in the theater.

Even If This Love Disappears from the World Tonight

A simple but good romance movie I have watched recently. The story is about a boy that made a fake confession to a girl as a bet. The girl suffers from a type of amnesia that resets her memory every night. Because of this she made a rule to not fall in love but continue this fake relationship. Despite her rule, they fall in love. The boy decided to make happy memories for her everyday.
